Managing Operations of Food and Beverage Industry with Microsoft Dynamics Ax

One of the renowned name in the food and beverage industry have implemented Microsoft Dynamics Ax ERP to transformed their business operations from the ground up. In an industry where product freshness, traceability, and inventory turnover are critical, Microsoft Dynamics Ax helped them gain complete visibility across our supply chain.

Gohra Technologies handled all new customization, automation of different processes, customization in different modules, integration with different third party tools like EDI.

Business Challenge

They faced several challenges across its operations. Disparate legacy systems managing finance, inventory, and production.

  • Lack of real-time visibility into inventory and production workflows.
  • Manual financial consolidation, causing delays in reporting and compliance.
  • Limited scalability to support new product lines and global expansion.
  • The leadership sought a modern ERP solution to streamline operations, improve decision-making, and support their growth strategy.

With Microsoft Dynamics Ax, Customer not only optimized their operations but also laid the digital foundation for scalable and intelligent process.

  • Enhanced Financial Visibility

    • Automated month-end closing
  • Scalability & Flexibility
    • Enabled multi-country operations with local compliance support
