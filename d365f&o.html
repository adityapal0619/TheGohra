Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations

Maximize financial visibility and profitability using D365 Finance and Operations ERP

What is Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations?

Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations is a cloud-based ERP system designed for large or diversified companies. It enables effective financial and inventory management through its integrated apps:

  • Dynamics 365 Finance: Budgeting, project management, financials, and accounting.
  • Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management: Engineering, manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution.

It delivers a comprehensive ERP experience for managing all business processes in a unified cloud environment.

Gohra Technologies specializes in successful Microsoft D365 implementations that truly deliver results.
