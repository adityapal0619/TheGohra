About GOHRA

Global Organization for Healthcare & Radiology Advancement

Our Mission

We empower global radiology professionals through education, advocacy, and innovation to improve patient care and health outcomes worldwide.

Our History

2010 – Founding of GOHRA

A group of passionate radiologists came together to start a movement focused on education and global outreach.

2015 – Global Expansion

GOHRA reached new heights, launching programs across 12 countries and partnering with leading healthcare institutes.

2020 – Digital Transformation

Virtual conferences, research platforms, and AI-based training modules became key initiatives.
